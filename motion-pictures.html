Personal Data Collections

In today's interconnected world, personal data is the fuel that drives many business operations. Companies collect it through cookies on websites, mobile apps, social media, online forms, loyalty programs, and even smart devices. Every digital interaction you make leaves a footprint. Narrated by NJ Rodrigo.

Importance of Privacy in Social Media

In today's digital age, social media plays a major role in how people communicate, share experiences, and stay connected. Platforms like Facebook, Instagram, Twitter(X), and TikTok have made it easy to post thoughts, photos, and personal updates with a wide audience. For many, social media is a space for self-expression, building relationships, and staying informed. Narrated by Khelvin Galvez.

Data Privacy

With the networked world that we have today, protecting one's personal information online is a necessity. All web behavior from social media status updates to online transactions leaves behind a trail of information that, if not protected, puts your identity and your finances at risk. Although online privacy is an international issue, the issues and the solutions are most relevant in the Philippines, where incidents like massive data breaches and fresh cyber cons have raised public consciousness. Narrated by Joseph Callos.
