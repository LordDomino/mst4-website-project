Data Privacy

With the networked world that we have today, protecting one's personal information online is a necessity. All web behavior from social media status updates to online transactions leaves behind a trail of information that, if not protected, puts your identity and your finances at risk. Although online privacy is an international issue, the issues and the solutions are most relevant in the Philippines, where incidents like massive data breaches and fresh cyber cons have raised public consciousness. Narrated by Joseph Callos.
